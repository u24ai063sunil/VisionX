## Delivery notes
- Say the two headline numbers slowly and clearly: **0.815 mAP** and **98% team consistency** — these are your credibility anchors, don't rush them.
- The 3:00–3:20 "honest limitation" line should land as confident engineering judgment, not an apology. Practice it a couple of times so the tone stays even.
- If recording narration separately from screen capture (recommended — see main recording guide), record the screen footage first at these exact timings, then lay voiceover on top and adjust cuts to match your natural speaking pace rather than forcing speech into rigid timestamps.
- Every time a specific number is spoken, make sure that number is also visible on screen at that exact moment (in the image itself or as a text overlay) — viewers retain on-screen numbers far better than spoken ones.
